Some good articles about great art

Below are links to some articles I read about great works of art. I highly enjoyed the articles (and the works that they are about)! I considered them well-written, and they greatly enriched my understanding of the artworks.

Sadly, most of the articles are behind a paywall — not every subject is like pure mathematics where preprints of nearly all published articles are freely available via arXiv. Nonetheless, if you're at university, your library will likely have access to the articles.

Films

  • Michelle Yates, Re-casting nature as feminist space in Mad Max: Fury Road (Science Fiction Film & Television, 2017)
  • Simone C. Drake, He Said Nothing: Sonic Space and the Production of Quietude in Barry Jenkins's Moonlight (Are You Entertained?: Black Popular Culture in the Twenty-First Century, 2020)
  • Stefanie K. Dunning, What is the Future? Weirdness and Black Time in Sorry to Bother You (Studies in the Fantastic, 2020)

Novels

  • Joanna Freer, Thomas Pynchon and the Black Panther Party: Revolutionary Suicide in Gravity's Rainbow, (Journal of American Studies, 2012)
